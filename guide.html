HOW TO SPACE SALAD 101

Thargoid combat is the endgame content of Elite:Dangerous and should not be taken lightly. Before you begin your fight with the thargoids, there are some things you need to take in mind!

1. Get Ready for Some L's

Thargoid combat is by no means easy and you should keep that in mind. The chances are you will lose your first combat with the interceptor and that is okay! Makes sure you can afford some rebuys on your pocket as you will be needing it very much.

2. Grab some friends

Although you can solo an interceptor, playing with others is always a better experience as not only they serve to protect you by drawing aggro, the also help you to deal damage. Making the fight faster.

3. Cold Orbit

This is the baasic and most effective strategy when dealing with thargoids. The definition is basically having a "cold" ship (temperature less than 30%) and orbiting a thargoid perpendicularly to it's vector. Avoiding it's shot and sometimes flawlessly defeat a thargoid.

4. The Right Guns

Most human weaponry will do minimal damage against these space salads. Your best bet to deal damage is by using either AX special weapons or Guardian weapons. With the later being the best out there.

5. Corrosive Damage

Apparently, this litte buggers can dish out a special type of damage called 'Corrosive Damage'. This type of damage phase through shields and directly hit your hull. It's effect also damages your hull slowly over time. The only way to remove this effect is by either using a decontamination limpet, dock on a station or megaship, or burn it through sheer heat by heating your shield to above 120%. Or 300% to instantly remove it.;
